Features

Smart Translation

6 translation engines with auto language detection, real-time translation, and alternative suggestions. Automatic fallback if one engine fails.

RTL Support

Automatic bidirectional text fixing in three levels: gentle, medium, and aggressive. Smart threshold and Persian font injection.
